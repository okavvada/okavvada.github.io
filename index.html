About me

This is where I share my thoughts on spatial analysis for water engineering and much more.

I am currently a postdoctoral student at the Lawrence Berkeley National Lab. Previously I completed my PhD degree at the University of California, Berkeley in Civil and Environmental Engineering. My research interests include systems-level analysis and life-cycle assessment of decentralized water reuse infrastructure. Particularly, I am interested in developing planning support tools to improve our usage of energy and water resources under the constraints of climate change.

My skills range from spatial modeling, control and optimization to modern data analysis and machine learning. My main research area is on integrated planning and systems-level analysis for urban water systems in order to minimize energy and environmental impacts.
