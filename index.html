About me

I am currently a postdoctoral student at the Lawrence Berkeley National Lab. I completed my PhD degree at the University of California, Berkeley in Civil and Environmental Engineering. My research interests revolve around spatial analysis, geospatial data science, databases and visualization. From my PhD career, postdoctoral research and several internships, I have gained experience in programming, data science, modeling, machine learning, working with databases and pipelines, vector and raster data particularly in integrating these tools with engineering analysis and optimal design.

My main research area during my PhD was using spatial data and systems-level modeling techniques develop software tools to assist the engineering decision making process. My research interests include decentralized water reuse infrastructure and improving our usage of energy and resources under the constraints of climate change.
